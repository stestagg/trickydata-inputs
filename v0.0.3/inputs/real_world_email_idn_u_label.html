real_world_email_idn_u_label

This uses the U-label presentation of the domain paired with its ASCII A-label form. It is valid internationalized SMTP mailbox syntax when SMTPUTF8 is available, but ASCII-only validators and transports may reject it despite the equivalent domain representation. RFC 6531 section 3.3 extends SMTP domains with U-labels, and RFC 5890 section 2.3.2.4 defines equivalence with A-labels: https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc6531.html#section-3.3 https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc5890.html#section-2.3.2.4

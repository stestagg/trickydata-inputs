real_world_email_domain_lowercase

This is the lowercase baseline for a domain-case pair. Mailbox domains follow DNS comparison rules and are case-insensitive, so the paired address denotes the same mailbox even though naive byte comparison says otherwise. RFC 5321 section 2.3.11 states that mailbox domains are not case-sensitive: https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc5321.html#section-2.3.11

invalid_utf8_extra_continuation

C2 A2 is a complete encoding of CENT SIGN (U+00A2), followed by an extra continuation byte. A decoder must not attach the third byte to the completed character or ignore it after successfully decoding the prefix. See the Unicode well-formed UTF-8 byte sequences in Table 3-7: https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode15.0.0/ch03.pdf

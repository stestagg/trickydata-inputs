real_world_email_confusable_cyrillic

The local-part uses Cyrillic U+0440, U+0430, U+0443, U+0440, and U+0430 followed by Latin `l`. RFC 6531 permits the non-ASCII atext under SMTPUTF8, but the address remains unequal to the visually similar ASCII pair.

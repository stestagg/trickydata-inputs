real_world_email_local_part_nfd

The visible `é` in the local-part is U+0065 followed by U+0301. RFC 6530 explicitly says unnormalized local-part strings are valid but may not work reliably, making this a trap for validators, account lookup, and systems that normalize identifiers without the destination's authority. RFC 6530 section 10.1 discusses valid unnormalized mailbox names: https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc6530.html#section-10.1

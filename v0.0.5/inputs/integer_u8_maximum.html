integer_u8_maximum

This is the maximum u8 value, 255, with every bit set. Adding 1 crosses the unsigned upper boundary and may trap, wrap to zero, saturate, or be widened before evaluation. Reinterpreting the same byte as i8 produces -1, making it useful for detecting signedness mistakes in storage, comparison, and conversion code.

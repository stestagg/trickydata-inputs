real_world_email_local_part_nfc

The local-part contains precomposed U+00E9 and looks the same as the paired decomposed spelling. Both are syntactically valid SMTPUTF8 addresses, but local-part interpretation belongs to the destination system; RFC 6530 recommends normalization or aliases rather than allowing intermediaries to assume equivalence. RFC 6530 section 10.1 discusses mailbox names and Unicode normalization: https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc6530.html#section-10.1

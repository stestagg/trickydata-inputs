integer_i8_minimum

This is the minimum i8 value, -128. Its positive magnitude, 128, is not representable as an i8, so unary negation and absolute-value operations may overflow, trap, wrap, or require a wider result type. Dividing it by -1 also has the mathematically correct but unrepresentable quotient 128, while subtracting 1 crosses the lower arithmetic boundary.

invalid_utf8_forbidden_leading_byte_fe

The byte 0xFE is never permitted in UTF-8. Older or overly permissive decoders may mistake it for a sequence starter left over from obsolete pre-RFC 3629 forms, while strict decoders must reject it immediately. See the Unicode well-formed UTF-8 byte sequences in Table 3-7: https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode15.0.0/ch03.pdf
